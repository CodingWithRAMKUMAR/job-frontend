How to Get Your First IT Job in India in 2026

📅 Published: April 10, 2026 | 6 min read

Landing your first IT job in India can feel impossible. Every job posting asks for "2-3 years experience" but how do you get that without a job? Here's a practical roadmap that actually works.

1. Build 2-3 Solid Projects

Companies don't expect professional experience, but they do expect you to know how to code. Build 2-3 complete projects that you can explain in detail. Examples: a weather app, a task manager, a portfolio site, or a small e-commerce site.

2. Master One Tech Stack

Don't try to learn everything. Pick one stack: MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) or Python with Django/Flask. Go deep, not wide. Companies hire for specific skills.

3. Create a Strong Resume

Keep it under 1 page. Include: technical skills, projects with links, internships (if any), education, and certifications. Remove irrelevant details like school achievements from 10th grade.

4. Apply Strategically

Apply to 10-15 jobs daily. Customize your resume for each role. Use ApplyMore to find fresher-friendly jobs in your city. Focus on quality over quantity.

5. Prepare for Interviews

Practice DSA problems on LeetCode (Easy/Medium level). Prepare to explain your projects for 10-15 minutes. Research the company before each interview.

6. Network on LinkedIn

Connect with HR and recruiters. Share your projects. Comment on industry posts. Many jobs come through referrals, not applications.

Remember: Every senior developer was once a fresher. Keep applying, keep learning, and don't give up!
