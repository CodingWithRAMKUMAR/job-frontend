About ApplyMore

🎯 Our Mission: Help every IT fresher in India find their first job within 3 months of graduation.

Who We Are

ApplyMore was founded by Ram, a developer who struggled to find entry-level IT jobs after graduation. I realized that freshers waste hours searching multiple job portals, only to find senior-level positions or irrelevant listings.

So I built ApplyMore — a free, automated platform that scrapes LinkedIn and Indeed daily, filters for 0-2 years experience, and shows jobs in major Indian cities including Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Gurugram, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, and Noida.

Why Trust ApplyMore?

✅ 100% Free — No paid plans, no hidden charges
✅ Daily Updates — We scrape every 12 hours
✅ No Spam — Only real job listings from verified companies
✅ Fresher-Focused — We filter out senior roles automatically
✅ Made in India — Built specifically for the Indian IT job market

How It Works

1. Our automated scraper searches LinkedIn and Indeed for 10+ IT roles (Data Analyst, Software Engineer, Java Developer, etc.)
2. Jobs are filtered by location (10+ Indian cities) and experience level
3. Duplicates are removed, and new jobs are added to our database
4. You see clean, organized listings — no clutter, no ads (yet!)

Our Commitment

ApplyMore will always remain free for job seekers. We may add non-intrusive ads in the future to cover server costs, but the core job search feature will never be paywalled.
