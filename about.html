🎯 My Story – The Reality of Being a Fresher in India (2026)

My name is Ram Kumar. I graduated in 2024 with a Computer Science degree. I was excited, hopeful, and ready to start my IT career. But the reality? It's been two years, and I'm still searching.

Every job portal shows positions that ask for "2-3 years experience" – how can a fresher get that? I've lost count of how many applications I've sent. 600? 700? The rejections pile up, the silence hurts, and sometimes I wonder if I'll ever get a chance.

📊 My Job Search Numbers (So Far):
• 600+ applications sent
• 550+ rejections or no response
• 50 interviews (some went well, some didn't)
• 0 offers yet – but I'm still trying every single day
• 100% determination to never give up

But here's what I realised: instead of just waiting for a job, I could build something that helps other freshers like me. Something that filters out the noise and shows only the roles we actually qualify for. That's how ApplyMore was born.

💡 Why ApplyMore Exists

I built ApplyMore because I was tired of seeing "Senior Software Engineer" posts when I was looking for entry-level roles. I built it because every fresher deserves a platform that understands their struggle – no paywalls, no premium plans, no fake hopes.

ApplyMore is my way of fighting back. While I continue my own job search, I'm making sure other freshers have a better tool than I did. Every job you see here is scraped, filtered, and presented with one goal: to help you find your first opportunity faster than I have.

👨‍💻 Who Am I Today?

I'm still a fresher. Still applying. Still getting rejections. But I'm also a self-taught developer who built a platform that serves thousands of job seekers. I may not have a job yet, but I have a mission: to help 10,000 IT freshers in India find their first job before I find mine.

Because your success is my motivation. Every time someone tells me they got hired through ApplyMore, it keeps me going. It tells me that even though I'm still searching, I'm making a difference.

📍 What We Cover (and Why)

Cities: Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Gurugram, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Noida – because freshers live everywhere, not just in metro cities.

Roles: Data Analyst, Software Engineer, Java Developer, Python Developer, DevOps Engineer, Data Scientist, Cyber Security, Apprenticeships – all entry-level, all suitable for 0-2 years experience.

Experience: Strictly 0-2 years. I've been burned by seeing "3+ years" jobs too many times. I won't let that happen to you.

⭐ My Promise to You (Even Before I Get a Job)

ApplyMore will ALWAYS be free. No premium plans, no hidden charges, no paywalls. I know what it's like to be a fresher with no money and no job. You shouldn't have to pay to find a job. Period.

🎯 My Goal (While I Keep Searching):
Help 10,000 IT freshers in India find their first job by December 2026. Even if I'm still looking for mine, I want you to find yours.
