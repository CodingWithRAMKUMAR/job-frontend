📖 My Story

Like many of you, I graduated with a degree in Computer Science and faced the same harsh reality: applying to hundreds of jobs, getting no response, or receiving automated rejections. I spent months learning new skills, building projects, and still struggled to find genuine fresher IT roles. That frustration pushed me to create ApplyMore – a platform that does exactly what I wished existed: a free, no‑nonsense job board that filters only real entry‑level IT jobs, updated automatically every 12 hours.

I built the entire scraper myself, pulling data from LinkedIn and Indeed, and added features like resume builder, interview prep, and a blog with actionable guides. Everything is free. No premium plans. No hidden charges. This is my way of giving back to the community that helped me stay motivated during my own job search.

🎯 Our Mission

ApplyMore’s mission is simple: help every IT fresher in India find their first job without spending money or time on fake listings. We believe that talent is evenly distributed, but opportunity is not. Our job is to bridge that gap with technology, transparency, and a genuine passion for helping freshers succeed.

🛠️ How ApplyMore Works

  • Automated scraping: Every 12 hours, we fetch the latest fresher IT jobs from LinkedIn and Indeed.
  • Strict filtering: We exclude senior roles, duplicate listings, and outdated posts.
  • User‑first design: No distracting ads (only Google AdSense to keep servers running).
  • Free tools: Resume builder, interview Q&A, and career blog – all at no cost.

❤️ Why I Do This

I still remember the day I received my first interview call after months of silence. That feeling of hope is what I want every fresher to experience. ApplyMore is not a business to me – it’s a mission. Every job posted, every resume downloaded, every question answered brings us one step closer to a world where no fresher feels left behind.
