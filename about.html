📖 About ApplyMore

Our Mission

ApplyMore was created to help IT freshers in India find their first job. After struggling to find entry-level positions myself, I built this platform to aggregate jobs from multiple sources and present them in a clean, easy-to-use interface.

Who We Serve

We focus on IT freshers and junior professionals with 0-2 years of experience in Indian cities including Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Gurugram, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, and Noida.

Why Trust Us

✓ 100% Free - No paid plans
✓ Daily Updates - Fresh jobs every 12 hours
✓ No Spam - Only real job listings
✓ Made in India - Built specifically for Indian IT job market

My Story

I'm Ram, a developer who spent months searching for my first IT job. I realized freshers waste hours on multiple job portals finding senior-level positions. So I built ApplyMore - automated, free, and focused only on entry-level IT jobs in India.
