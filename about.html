Our Mission

The IT job market in India is tough for freshers. Despite having the skills, many graduates struggle to find their first opportunity. We believe every talented individual deserves a fair chance.

ApplyMore bridges this gap by:

  • Curating genuine opportunities – We filter out fake postings and focus on real jobs
  • Providing career guidance – Resume tips, interview prep, and skill development advice
  • Building a supportive community – Connect with peers, share experiences, and grow together
  • Staying completely free – No hidden costs, no premium tiers, just pure value
