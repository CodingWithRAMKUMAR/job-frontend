How to Get Your First IT Job in India

Landing your first tech job can feel overwhelming. Here are 5 practical tips to help you stand out.

1. Build a Strong Foundation

Master at least one programming language (Python, Java, or JavaScript) and understand basic data structures. Online platforms like freeCodeCamp, Codecademy, and YouTube are great resources.

2. Create a Portfolio

Show your skills by building 2‑3 projects (e.g., a weather app, a to‑do list, or a small e‑commerce site). Host them on GitHub and include the links in your resume.

3. Target Fresher‑Friendly Job Boards

Use ApplyMore – it aggregates entry‑level IT jobs across India and updates daily. Go to ApplyMore to find the latest openings.

4. Polish Your Resume

Keep it one page, highlight projects and internships, and tailor it for each application. Avoid generic phrases; focus on what you can do.

5. Network and Stay Updated

Follow companies on LinkedIn, attend virtual tech meetups, and connect with peers. Many jobs come through referrals.
