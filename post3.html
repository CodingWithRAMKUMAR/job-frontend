Top 20 Interview Questions for IT Freshers (With Answers)

📅 Published: April 11, 2026 | 8 min read

After analyzing 50+ fresher interview experiences, here are the most common questions asked by Indian IT companies in 2026.

Technical Questions (Most Common)

1. What is your favorite programming language and why?
Answer honestly about what you know best. Mention specific features you like.

2. Explain one project from your resume in detail.
Prepare a 5-minute explanation for each project. Include: problem, solution, tech stack, challenges faced.

3. What is the difference between == and .equals() in Java?
== compares references, .equals() compares values. (For Python: is vs ==)

4. Explain OOP concepts with real examples.
Encapsulation, Inheritance, Polymorphism, Abstraction - use car/bank account examples.

5. Write a simple SQL query.
Practice SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY. Most common: "Find the second highest salary"

HR Questions

6. Tell me about yourself.
Keep it to 60 seconds: education → skills → projects → why you're interested in this role.

7. Why should we hire you?
Focus on: "I have the skills you need, I'm a quick learner, and I'm passionate about this field."

8. What are your salary expectations?
Research market rate for freshers (3-6 LPA for IT roles in India). Say "I'm open to discussion based on market standards."

9. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
"Growing technically, taking on more responsibility, and contributing to impactful projects."

Tips for Success

✓ Research the company before interview
✓ Prepare 3-4 questions to ask them
✓ Practice with mock interviews (use friends or online platforms)
✓ Send a thank you email within 24 hours
